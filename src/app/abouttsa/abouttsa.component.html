Skyline TSA

Here at Skyline, TSA’s motto of “Learning to lead in a technical world” is something that we take to heart. Although the Skyline chapter isn’t huge, we are chock-full of talented and creative technological leaders. Skyline TSA is also home to a large, dedicated VEX team.

History

The Skyline TSA chapter was founded in 2014 by our advisor Kenneth Kapptie. Since its inception, Skyline has had great success - with achievements including placing multiple times at both State and National competitions, and being Utah’s Tech Bowl state champions for the past three years.
