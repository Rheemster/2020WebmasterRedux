Courses

Skyline has a huge array of CTE course options for any and all students, including Advanced Graphics Communications, Computer Programming I & II (IB), Digital Media & Digital Media II, Computer Technology, Exploring Computer Science, Foods, Graphics, Law Enforcement, Sports Medicine CE, Woodworking, and Yearbook.

Kenneth Kapptie

As the advisor for and the founder of the Skyline TSA Chapter, as well as the programming teacher at Skyline High, Kapptie has provided a great deal to the students in the CTE program. Students have made a number of programs that have showcased all they learned under his guidance, such as the popular Bearly Dancing game by Oliver Flatt.

Shawn Kennedy

As a teacher that enables students to get real-world experience making products like t-shirts, stickers, and other physical realizations of digital design, Kennedy has done a great deal for the CTE program. He brought with him to Skyline all his years of experience as an entrepreneur and a worker in the industry and has imparted much of that knowledge into his students. In addition to the knowledge, Kennedy has given CTE students a taste of the work ethic necessary for the jobs his classes give experience, letting students get a real taste in the industry.
